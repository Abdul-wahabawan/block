🧩 What is Block Blast?

Block Blast is a popular tile puzzle game where players place different-shaped blocks onto a grid to form and clear full rows or squares. It's easy to start, but it gets harder as the board fills up. Smart planning, quick decisions, and spatial skills are key to surviving longer and scoring higher.

🎯 How to Score Higher in Block Blast?

  • Think ahead: Always visualize how the next blocks might fit.
  • Clear the center: Keep the middle of the board as open as possible.
  • Eliminate tricky shapes first: Prioritize placing odd blocks early.
  • Maximize chain clears: Clearing multiple lines at once boosts your score.
